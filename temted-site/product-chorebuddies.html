How it works

Up and running in five minutes

  1. 01

    Create your family

    Add parents and kids in one tap. Each person gets their own profile, avatar, and age-appropriate view.

  2. 02

    Pick chores from the library

    Start with our curated lists by age — or build your own. Set how often and how many points each chore is worth.

  3. 03

    Set rewards that matter

    Movie nights, screen time, allowance, weekend outings — you decide. Kids see exactly what their points unlock.

  4. 04

    Watch the habits stick

    Streaks, badges, and gentle reminders do the heavy lifting. You get a calmer house and a clear weekly view.

FAQ

Common questions

What ages is ChoreBuddies for?+

ChoreBuddies is designed for kids ages 4 to 16. Younger kids see a simpler view with bigger icons; older kids unlock more independence and customisation.

Do I need a phone for each child?+

No. Kids can use a shared device, log in on a tablet, or even a kid-mode kiosk on the family fridge. Parents control everything from their own phone.

How do you handle privacy?+

ChoreBuddies is COPPA and GDPR-K aligned. We don't sell data, we don't run third-party ads, and we limit data collection to what's strictly needed to run the app.

Can grandparents or co-parents join?+

Yes. Family Plus supports up to 10 members across two households, with role-based permissions for parents, guardians, and observers.

When does the Schools Edition launch?+

We're piloting with select schools through 2026 and aim for a full release in the second half of the year. Get in touch if you'd like to join the waitlist.
