The sensor

Clips on in seconds. Works on any brace.

The OrthoLuma sensor is a small, lightweight device that attaches to virtually any orthodontic or orthopaedic brace. It passively measures wear time and syncs wirelessly to the app - no buttons, no charging ritual, no friction.

  • ✓ Universal clip - fits most brace types
  • ✓ Weeks of battery life on a single charge
  • ✓ Bluetooth Low Energy sync - no manual data entry
  • ✓ Splash-resistant for day-to-day wear

How it works

Up and tracking in three steps

  1. 01

    Attach the sensor

    Clip the OrthoLuma sensor onto the brace. It pairs to the app via Bluetooth in under a minute - no tools, no fuss.

  2. 02

    Track wear time automatically

    The sensor logs every minute the brace is on. The family app shows live data; the clinician console updates daily.

  3. 03

    Share insights with your clinician

    Grant your clinician access from the app. They'll see verified wear-time trends and receive automated reports ahead of each appointment.
