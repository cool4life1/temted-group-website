Why we exist

Make building useful things less expensive — in every sense of the word.

Most teams don't lack ideas. They lack the people, patterns and platforms to ship reliably. TemTed Group exists to compress that distance — by building products people actually use, training engineers and product folks who can build them, and partnering with organisations who need a steady hand.

What we believe

Four values that show up in every project.

01

Useful beats clever

We optimise for outcomes a real person can feel — not for novelty, jargon, or vanity dashboards.

02

Senior, small teams

We staff problems with experienced people in tight teams. Less hand-off, more ownership, faster decisions.

03

Transfer, don't trap

Whether we're building or training, our job is to leave your team stronger — not dependent on us.

04

Honest by default

We say what we see. Hard truths, kindly delivered, beat polite fictions every single time.

Our story

From a single product to a focused group.

  1. 2019

    The first build

    TemTed starts as a two-person studio shipping web apps for early-stage founders in Lagos.

  2. 2021

    Academy launches

    First cohort of TemTed Academy graduates — built to solve our own hiring pipeline, opened to the wider ecosystem.

  3. 2022

    ChoreBuddies goes live

    Our first owned product launches and crosses 50,000 households inside 18 months.

  4. 2024

    Group structure

    Three practices — Products, Academy, Consulting — formalised under TemTed Group.

  5. 2026

    Where we are now

    Twelve senior practitioners. Two products in market. Cohort 06 of the Academy live. New consulting partnerships across fintech, edtech and public sector.
