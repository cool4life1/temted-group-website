Our story

From consulting roots to a growing digital ecosystem.

  1. Start

    TemTed Group begins

    TemTed Group was established in Perth, Western Australia, with an initial focus on digital consulting, implementation, and delivery support.

  2. Grow

    Services and capabilities expand

    Over time, the business grew beyond consulting into broader digital services, training, and practical solution delivery across different sectors.

  3. Academy

    TemTed Academy launches

    TemTed Academy was introduced to support learners and professionals through practical training, guided programmes, and career-focused development.

  4. Product

    New digital products emerge

    TemTed began expanding into owned products and platforms, including tools designed for families, healthcare, mobility, and other real-world needs.

  5. Now

    Where we are today

    Today, TemTed Group is evolving into a connected ecosystem of apps, learning platforms, and digital services designed to support both individuals and organisations.
