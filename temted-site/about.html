About TemTed Group

A Perth-based studio for builders, learners and operators.

Why we exist

Make building useful things less expensive — in every sense of the word.

Most teams don't lack ideas — they lack the people, patterns and platforms to ship reliably. TemTed Group exists to compress that distance: design and build software people actually use, train career-ready professionals through TemTed Academy, and partner with organisations who need a steady, senior hand from discovery through to launch and support.

What we believe

Four values that show up in every project.

01

Useful beats clever

We optimise for outcomes a real person can feel — not for novelty, jargon, or vanity dashboards.

02

Senior, small teams

We staff problems with experienced people in tight teams. Less hand-off, more ownership, faster decisions.

03

Transfer, don't trap

Whether we're building or training, our job is to leave your team stronger — not dependent on us.

04

Honest by default

We say what we see. Hard truths, kindly delivered, beat polite fictions every single time.

Our story

From a single product to a focused group.

  1. Start

    The studio is founded

    TemTed Group is established in Perth, Western Australia — a small senior team taking on design and development work.

  2. Grow

    Services expand

    The practice widens beyond build work into branding, training and ongoing support — anchored around delivery quality.

  3. Academy

    TemTed Academy launches

    Career-track programs in Business Analysis, Scrum, Product Ownership and Salesforce — live cohorts with mentorship and real outcomes.

  4. Product

    ChoreBuddies goes live

    Our first owned consumer product launches — helping families build consistent routines through fun, interactive checklists and rewards.

  5. Now

    Where we are today

    Six service practices. Active partnerships across healthcare, fitness, professional services and not-for-profit. New work in telematics and applied AI.
